When I started

I actually started taking pictures not too long ago (if that isn't already obvious from my pictures). I got my first and only camera in Feburary of 2024. It's a Nikon D80 and basically as starter as you can get without breaking the bank.

Right now, I really don't take pictures of anything because during the winter I'm almost never outside. As you can see though, my main thing is boat days. I love being able to capture the beauty of the outdoors and also make it a way my friend can enjoy too from me getting some good pictures of them. When I first got my camera and was messing around with it I would get better and practice by capturing my animals.
